## Incorrect or Unverifiable Claims

### 1. "Adobe Partner" Status - UNVERIFIED

The agent file claims "Adobe official partner/educator" but research could not confirm a formal Adobe partnership. While McKinnon uses and teaches Adobe products (Premiere Pro, Lightroom), I found no evidence of an official Adobe Ambassador or Partner status like his documented Canon Ambassador role.

**Recommendation:** Change "Adobe Partner" to "Uses and teaches Adobe Lightroom and Premiere Pro" or remove the specific claim.

### 2. Specific Slider Value Ranges - PARTIALLY VERIFIED

The exact slider values (e.g., "Blacks: +15 to +25", "Grain: 22, Size 30, Roughness 60") are reasonable approximations but are not directly documented from McKinnon himself. His presets are proprietary and sold commercially.

**Recommendation:** Add a disclaimer that values are "typical ranges for this style" rather than "Peter McKinnon's exact settings."

### 3. "Edit the Feeling, Not the Photo" - ATTRIBUTABLE BUT NOT EXACT QUOTE

This philosophy aligns with McKinnon's documented approach ("photos to mean something, to transcend just 'another shot' and become undeniable moments of clear storytelling and emotion") but the exact phrase "Edit the feeling, not the photo" was not found in search results.

**Recommendation:** Keep the philosophy but note it's paraphrased from his teaching style.
